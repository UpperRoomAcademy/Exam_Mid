One day, M was jumping on his bed when he fell and broke his middle leg! "Ouch!" cried M. His friend N quickly called for help. They rushed to the hospital where Doctor O wrapped up M's leg in a white bandage. Nurse N gave him a lollipop and said, "No more jumping on the bed!" M smiled and promised to be more careful. Soon his leg felt much better, and he thanked Doctor O and Nurse N for taking such good care of him.
